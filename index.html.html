Introduction

Nino Kuni 2 is a Action Role playing game that is the sequel to Nino Kuni: Wrath of the White Witch that is filled with creatures, weaponry, and magic in a graphically mesmerizing open world.

Next

Plot

Nino Kuni 2 is centered around a young prince named Evan, who escapes a coup d'etat of his father kingdom, Ding Dong Dell, with a mysterious figure named Roland. From there, Evan and Roland goes out into world, accumulating strength and friends in order to create his own kingdom and create peace by Unifying all others countries under one banner. But of the many challenges he faces, his greatest one is battling the dark evil that lurks behind the shadows.

Next

Content

The content can be broken down into two categories, combat and game modes. During combat, you are allowed to fight enemies in a party of three. Those three in the party can use any variation of weapons, and magic to defeat your enemies. After you battles, you can engage yourself in other activities like Kingdom building, Time constricted dungeons, Army battles, and side quests to keep you entertained on your adventure.

Summary

Nino kuni 2’s simplistic yet entertaining story that takes you on a journey through a young prince’s transition from the despair of losing his kingdom to the success of creating a new one. This game is perfect for people new and old to the series, because the story takes on a different path of the previous game, provides players with a open world to explore, provides easy controls, and many game modes to pass the time while going through the main story. I highly reccomend you giving it a try!
